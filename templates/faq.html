Frequently Asked Questions

Limits

Each user is limited to having at most 10 associated Twitter accounts.

Each Twitter account is limited to 25 deletions per hour. This means a sustained rate greater than 25 posts per hour will force Media Maid to 'fall behind' and leave some posts in the stream.

Media Maid will not delete any posts made before you activated an associated Twitter account. This means that if you choose a 24-hour window, it will take 24 hours before you see the service working.
