Welcome to Media Maid

Media Maid is a social media tool that helps you manage your online presence by deleting posts after a specified time limit. (The service is limited to managing Twitter accounts for the time being.)

The inspiration for this project came from Danah Boyd's blog post "Risk Reduction Strategies on Facebook". In this post, Danah describes how several (very innovative) teens manage their online accounts. In addition to a Facebook "super-logoff" technique, Danah outlines the following strategy:

"Shamika doesn't deactivate her Facebook profile but she does delete every wall message, status update, and Like shortly after it's posted. She'll post a status update and leave it there until she's ready to post the next one or until she's done with it. Then she'll delete it from her profile. When she's done reading a friend's comment on her page, she'll delete it. Sheâ€™ll leave a Like up for a few days for her friends to see and then delete it."

This strategy won't be applicable to everyone, but it is an interesting and technically feasible use case that this site tries to address.
